Innovation and technological adaptation are necessary in order for liberal arts colleges to thrive. Despite this need, many liberal arts college presidents are resisting the need for progress. Donald Eastman, President of Eckerd College in St. Petersburg, FL, routinely writes Op­Ed's for the Tampa Bay Times which defend the value of the liberal arts while criticizing the low quality experience of online education.
